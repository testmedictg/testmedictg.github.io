Our Mission

TestMediCTG aims to bring transparency to healthcare costs in Chittagong by providing accurate, up-to-date information about diagnostic test prices across the city's leading diagnostic centers.

We believe that everyone should have access to clear pricing information before making healthcare decisions, helping patients save money and make informed choices.



The Problem

Chittagong has varying test prices across different diagnostic centers, with hidden details and lack of transparency. Patients often pay different prices for the same test without knowing they could get it for less elsewhere.



Our Solution

TestMediCTG provides transparent, verified online data updated every 30 days. We show comparative pricing from all major diagnostic centers in Chittagong, helping you make informed decisions about your healthcare spending.



What We Do

TestMediCTG is an information-only platform. We do not book tests or appointments. Our focus is solely on providing accurate, up-to-date pricing information to help you compare costs across different diagnostic centers in Chittagong.

Our team regularly verifies prices with diagnostic centers to ensure the information we provide is current and reliable.
